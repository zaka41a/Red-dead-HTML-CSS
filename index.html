Gameplay

Immersive western experience: exploration, survival, and cinematic storytelling.

Open World & Exploration

Travel freely across a vast frontier with your horse, discover towns, camps and hidden paths. Hunt, fish, craft, and engage with dynamic events that make the world feel alive.

Combat & Choices

Realistic firefights, duels and stealth encounters are combined with meaningful decisions. Your actions shape your honor, relationships, and ultimately the story’s outcome.

Tips for Beginners

Helpful advice to get started right.

  1. Care for your horse and keep supplies handy.
  2. Watch for visual cues (smoke, shouts, side paths).
  3. Optimize your inventory: ammo, tonics, baits.
